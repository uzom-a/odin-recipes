Description

This quick and flavorful Nigerian-style Indomie noodles recipe combines the simplicity of instant noodles with fresh ingredients like onions, tomatoes, carrots, and a touch of chili for a spicy kick. Boiled together to blend the flavors, the noodles are then seasoned with the iconic Indomie spice packets, enhanced optionally with an egg fried in vegetable oil for extra protein and richness. This dish is a beloved, easy-to-make meal that's perfect for a fast, satisfying breakfast or snack any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Indomie instant noodles (any flavor)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (chopped)
  • 1 small carrot (grated or chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon oil (vegetable or palm oil)
  • Seasoning cubes or powder (Maggi or Knorr)

Steps

  1. Boil about 2 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, carrot, and chili to the boiling water. Let it cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. In a separate pan, heat oil, then fry an egg (if using).
  5. Add the seasoning packets from the Indomie and mix well into the noodles. Add salt or seasoning cubes if needed.
  6. Combine the fried egg with the noodles or mix it in.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
